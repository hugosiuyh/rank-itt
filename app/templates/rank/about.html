Hi {{name}}.

Amateur pingpong players deserves some competitiveness like professionals, don't you think? We need your help (and a pinch of honesty lol) to figure out if amateur pingpong can be more than just social playing.

Game instructions:

  • Each game is 11 point.
  • Switch serves every 2 points.
  • Deuce until there's a winner.
  • No draws allowed.

Submission instructions:

  • Submit the scores on the "Add" tab after every games.
  • The other player must accept the scores on their "Profile" tab.
  • Only until then, the score is published and reflected in the ranking.
  • No draws allowed.

About the ranking algorithm

  • Conventional league format is deterministic point-counting.
  • It requires all players to play the same amount of games.
  • Instead, we use Microsoft's TrueSkill like in Xbox games, e.g. GTA or Halo to determine your skill.
  • It's a Bayesian inference algorithm that models each player with a Normal distribution.
  • With mean = skill and sigma = uncertainty.
